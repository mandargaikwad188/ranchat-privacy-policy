Privacy Policy for RanChat

Last Updated: September 14, 2024

Quick Summary: RanChat is an anonymous chat app that prioritizes your privacy. We collect minimal data needed for functionality, encrypt all communications, and never share your personal information. Your messages are automatically deleted after 24 hours for maximum privacy protection.

1. Introduction

RanChat ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, anonymous chat experience. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RanChat (the "App").

This policy is designed to comply with international privacy laws including GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Store requirements. We follow the principle of data minimization, collecting only what is necessary for our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Mandatory Information

  • Device ID: Unique device identifier for anonymous user identification
  • IP Address: Automatically collected for security and service provision
  • App Version: For compatibility and update purposes

2.2 Optional Information

  • Profile Information: Avatar selection, gender, age preferences (if provided)
  • Location Data: Approximate location for matching nearby users (only with explicit consent)
  • Phone Number: For age verification in certain regions (if required by law)

2.3 Chat Data

  • Messages: Text messages, images, videos, and audio content
  • Chat History: Conversation data with automatic deletion after 24 hours
  • Media Files: Photos, videos, and audio messages (automatically deleted after 2-3 days)

2.4 Usage and Performance Data

  • App Activity: Feature usage, interaction patterns (anonymized)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data to improve app stability
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics for service improvement

4. Data Collection, Sharing, and Handling Procedures

4.1 Location Information

We collect location information only with your explicit consent to enhance services and provide location-based matching. This information is:

  • Processed in a form that does not allow personal identification
  • Not disclosed under any circumstances
  • Immediately destroyed upon withdrawal of consent

4.2 Media Collection and Use

The application includes features for users to upload images, videos, and audio messages:

  • Profile Images: Automatically deleted within 2-3 days or upon account deletion
  • Shared Media: Automatically deleted after 2-3 days for privacy protection
  • Chat Messages: Automatically deleted after 24 hours

4.3 Security Practices

  • All data in transit is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Data at rest is encrypted and stored securely
  • Users can request data deletion using the app's withdrawal feature
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5.1 Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in app functionality:

  • Supabase: Database and real-time chat functionality
  • Firebase (Google): Push notifications and analytics
  • Google AdMob: Advertisement display
  • Google Play Billing: In-app purchase processing

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes or government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, or safety
  • Prevent harm to users or the community
  • Investigate violations of our terms of service

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Data Control Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities

7.2 Privacy Settings

  • Location Services: Enable/disable location-based features
  • Notifications: Control push notification preferences
  • Profile Visibility: Control who can see your profile
  • Data Sharing: Opt-out of analytics and advertising

8. Children's Privacy

RanChat is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.
